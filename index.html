Famous Filipino Cuisine

Photo Origin and Info
Sisig refers to a finely chopped pork dish of Filipino origin. This recipe uses pork belly and maskara (pig face). It is popular in the Philippines and is now gaining popularity worldwide. It can be consumed as an appetizer and a main dish.
Pancit Canton Guisado is a savory vegetable filled Filipino noodle dish where canton noodles are stir fried with chicken, chicken liver, cabbage, carrot, green beans, squid balls in a sauce made with soy sauce, oyster sauce. garlic, onion and fish sauce
Adobo The Filipino Adobo is a marinated pork in soy sauce and crushed garlic. And if you would like, vinegar is something you can add as a marinade ingredient.
Lechon Kawali is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient. Other proteins and seafood can also be used. Beef, shrimp, fish are commonly used to cook sinigang.
